Preparation Time

  • Total: 1 hour
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 1 kg
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Ginger paste - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cooking oil - 3 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish

Instructions

  1. Clean the chicken and cut it into medium-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, garlic paste, ginger paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to form a marinade.
  3. Marinate the chicken pieces in the mixture and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and add the marinated chicken. Cook on medium heat until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
