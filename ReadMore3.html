Salmon Salad Recipe

A light and refreshing salad with grilled salmon, avocado, and fresh vegetables.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 20-25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 10-15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets - 2 (about 6 ounces each)
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Spinach - 4 cups
  • Cucumber - 1/2, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes - 1 cup, halved
  • Red onion - 1/4 cup, thinly sliced
  • Avocado - 1, sliced
  • Lemon juice - 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Honey - 1 teaspoon
  • Dijon mustard - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill or broiler.
  2. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or broil salmon for 4-6 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. In a large bowl, combine spinach, cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
  7. Top with grilled salmon and avocado slices.
  8. Serve immediately.
