Sheer Khurma Recipe

A fragrant and rich milk-based dessert, Sheer Khurma is a popular treat during Eid.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 30-40 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 20-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Milk - 2 liters
  • Semolina (Sooji) - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup (or to taste)
  • Ghee - 4 tablespoons
  • Cashews - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Almonds - 1/4 cup, sliced
  • Raisins - 1/4 cup
  • Pistachios - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Saffron strands - a pinch
  • Cardamom pods - 4-5, crushed
  • Coconut flakes - 1/4 cup (optional)
  • Rose water - 1-2 teaspoons (optional)
  • Milk powder - 1/4 cup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add semolina and roast until fragrant and lightly golden brown, stirring continuously.
  3. Add milk to the pot and bring to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent sticking.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the semolina is cooked through and the milk has thickened.
  5. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  6. Add cashews, almonds, raisins, pistachios, saffron, cardamom, and coconut flakes (if using).
  7. Simmer for another 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors have infused.
  8. Stir in milk powder (if using) and rose water (if using).
  9. Cook for 1-2 more minutes, then remove from heat.
  10. Serve warm or chilled.
