Preparation Time

  • Total: 45 minutes
  • Preparation: 25 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • For the Manchurian Balls:
  • Cauliflower florets - 1 cup
  • Carrot - 1 medium, grated
  • Green beans - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Spring onions - 2, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Corn flour - 1/4 cup
  • Oil - for frying
  • For the Manchurian Sauce:
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic cloves - 2, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece, minced
  • Onion - 1 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies - 2-3, chopped
  • Soy sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Tomato ketchup - 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Corn flour slurry - 1 tbsp corn flour + 2 tbsp water
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - 1/2 cup
  • Spring onions - for garnish

Instructions

  1. Make the Manchurian Balls:
  2. Combine cauliflower, carrot, green beans, spring onions, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add corn flour and mix well to form a dough.
  4. Shape the dough into small balls.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the balls until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Make the Manchurian Sauce:
  8. Heat oil in a pan and add garlic and ginger. Saute for a minute.
  9. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until softened.
  10. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, and salt. Mix well.
  11. Add corn flour slurry and water. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
  12. Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and toss well to coat.
  13. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
