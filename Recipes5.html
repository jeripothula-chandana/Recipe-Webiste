Thali Recipe

Preparation Time

  • Total: 1 hour
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

    For the Filling:

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

    • For the Dough:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour (optional, for a more flaky crust)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil, plus extra for frying
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

    1. Heat oil in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
    3. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook for a further minute.
    4. Add mashed potatoes, green peas, and salt. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
    5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and set aside to cool.
