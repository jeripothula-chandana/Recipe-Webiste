Preparation Time

  • Total: 2-3 hours
  • Preparation: 1-2 hours
  • Cooking: 1-2 hours

Ingredients

  • For Dal Makhani: Urad dal, rajma, butter, cream, spices
  • For Paneer Butter Masala: Paneer, tomatoes, onions, ginger-garlic paste, cream, spices
  • For Palak Paneer: Spinach, paneer, ginger-garlic paste, spices
  • For Jeera Rice: Basmati rice, cumin seeds, ghee
  • For Roti/Naan: Whole wheat flour/all-purpose flour, water, oil
  • For Raita: Yogurt, cucumber, tomato, spices
  • For Papad/Pappadum: Papad, oil
  • For Salad: Mixed vegetables, dressing
  • For Pickle/Chutney: Mango pickle, green chutney, etc.

Instructions

  1. Prepare Dal Makhani: Soak urad dal and rajma overnight. Cook until tender. Temper with ghee and spices. Add cream and butter.
  2. Prepare Paneer Butter Masala: Blend tomatoes and onions. Cook with ginger-garlic paste and spices. Add paneer and cream.
  3. Prepare Palak Paneer: Blend spinach. Cook with ginger-garlic paste and spices. Add paneer.
  4. Cook Jeera Rice: Wash and soak rice. Heat ghee and add cumin seeds. Add rice and cook until done.
  5. Make Roti/Naan: Knead dough. Roll out and cook on a tawa/grill.
  6. Make Raita: Whisk yogurt. Add chopped cucumber, tomato, and spices.
  7. Fry Papad: Fry papad until crispy.
  8. Assemble Thali: Arrange all the dishes on a platter.
