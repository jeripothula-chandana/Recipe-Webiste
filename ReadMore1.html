Bolognese Recipe

A classic Italian meat sauce, Bolognese is rich, flavorful, and perfect served over pasta.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 2 hours
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 1 large, chopped
  • Carrot - 1 large, chopped
  • Celery - 2 stalks, chopped
  • Ground beef - 1 lb
  • Ground pork - 1 lb
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Tomato paste - 2 tbsp
  • Red wine - 1 cup
  • Crushed tomatoes - 28 oz can
  • Chicken broth - 1 cup
  • Worcestershire sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Dried oregano - 1 tsp
  • Dried basil - 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Parmesan cheese - for serving
  • Pasta - for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrot, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and pork. Cook, breaking up the meat with a spoon, until browned.
  4. Drain off excess fat.
  5. Stir in milk and cook until evaporated.
  6. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  7. Add red wine, crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  9. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  10. Serve Bolognese sauce over cooked pasta and top with Parmesan cheese.
