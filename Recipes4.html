Thali Recipe

Preparation Time

  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add heavy cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
