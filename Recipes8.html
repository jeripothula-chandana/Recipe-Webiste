Preparation Time

  • Total: 4-5 hours (including fermentation)
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 5-7 minutes per dosa

Ingredients

  • Rice - 1 cup
  • Urad Dal (Black Gram) - 1/4 cup
  • Fenugreek Seeds (Methi) - 1 tsp
  • Water - As needed for soaking and grinding
  • Salt - To taste
  • Oil - For cooking
  • Ghee - For brushing
  • Optional: Potato filling, chutney, sambar

Instructions

  1. Soak and Grind Batter:
  2. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-5 hours.
  3. Grind the soaked rice and urad dal separately to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Combine the rice and urad dal batter in a large bowl.
  5. Add salt and mix well. Cover and let the batter ferment for 8-12 hours at room temperature.
  6. Make the Dosa:
  7. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  8. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the tawa and spread it thinly in a circular motion using the back of the ladle.
  9. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges.
  10. Cook until the edges turn golden brown and the dosa is cooked through.
  11. Flip the dosa and cook for a few seconds on the other side.
  12. Serve hot with your favorite chutney, sambar, or potato filling.
